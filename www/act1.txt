ACT 1 — “THE LOST PHONE”
Product Design Breakdown (Detailed PM-Level Structure)

Act 1 is NOT about horror yet.

Its job is:

build realism,
create emotional curiosity,
establish the owner as a believable human,
quietly plant mystery clues.

The player should initially think:

“This feels like someone’s real phone.”

If Act 1 fails realism,
the entire game fails.

ACT 1 GOAL

The player must:

Unlock the phone
Learn who Aarav is
Understand something is wrong
Discover his final investigation

Reach the first major twist:

“You should not have picked up this phone.”
